Photography Collection

Capturing school life and basketball moments

Project Overview

This collection showcases my photography work capturing authentic moments from school life and basketball games. The images document real experiences and emotions during my academic journey.

Technical Details

  • Shot with DSLR and mirrorless cameras
  • Natural lighting and candid compositions
  • Post-processing in Lightroom and Photoshop
  • Action shots captured with high shutter speeds
  • Portraits with shallow depth of field

Creative Process

My approach focuses on capturing genuine moments rather than staged scenes. For basketball photography, I positioned myself to capture the intensity of the game while maintaining clean compositions.

The school life series documents everyday moments that collectively tell the story of student experiences, from classroom interactions to campus environments.
