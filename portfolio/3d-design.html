3D Exterior Design

Institutional building visualization

Project Overview

This project involved creating a detailed 3D exterior design of my former educational institution. The goal was to visualize the campus architecture with realistic materials and lighting.

Technical Details

  • Modeled in Blender and 3DS Max
  • High-poly modeling for detailed architecture
  • PBR materials for realistic surfaces
  • Global illumination lighting
  • Post-processing in Photoshop

Development Process

I began with reference photos and architectural plans of the existing buildings. After blocking out the basic shapes, I refined the models with proper proportions and architectural details.

The texturing phase involved creating custom materials that matched the real-world surfaces. Final renders were composited with environmental elements and post-processed for maximum realism.
