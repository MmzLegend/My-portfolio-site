RELEC Overview

RELEC is a conceptual brand built on the ideas of continuity, unity, and excellence. The logo was designed using the ancient symbol of the Ouroborosâ€”a serpent eating its own tail, symbolizing eternal cycles and unbroken unity. The design draws stylistic inspiration from the crest of Madrid, reflecting elements of royalty, legacy, and power.

Design Intent

  • Symbolism: The Ouroboros emphasizes continuous growth and eternal strength
  • Style: Royal insignia inspiration conveys prestige and authority
  • Typography: Clean and modern, balancing the historic symbol with a contemporary identity

Tools & Process

Designed in Adobe Illustrator as a fully scalable vector. The process included concept sketching, vector refinement, and visual mockups to test versatility across branding assets.
