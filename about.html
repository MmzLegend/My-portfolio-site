About Me

Hello, I'm Muhammad Mukhtar Zikirullahi

I graduated with a BSc in Information Technology from Middlesex University Dubai and also hold an Advanced Diploma in Multimedia. While my academic background leans toward IT, my heart has always been in the creative world. From 3D modeling and design to photography and videography, I’ve always found joy in blending technology with creativity.

My journey began with 3D modeling during my diploma days, where I started by creating simple objects and environments. University introduced me to the coding and development side of tech, expanding my toolkit and pushing me to explore how I could merge both the technical and the artistic. Now, as I prepare to pursue my master’s degree, I’m even more driven to combine these two worlds into something powerful and unique.

Since graduating, I’ve worked across various creative roles — including videographer, photographer, graphic designer — and I'm currently serving as an IT Officer at Resource Centre for Human Rights and Civic Education CHRICED. Each experience continues to shape how I see digital storytelling and design, and I’m constantly pushing boundaries to deliver experiences that are both visually compelling and technically sound.
