Project Overview

This architectural visualization project recreates my former educational institution with photorealistic detail. The complete exterior modeling includes accurate proportions, materials, and environmental lighting to achieve a true-to-life representation.

Software Used

AutoCAD

For precise technical drawings and layouts

3DS Max

3D modeling and scene composition

V-Ray

Photorealistic rendering and lighting

Technical Details

  • Precise modeling based on architectural blueprints
  • High-poly detailing for realistic facade elements
  • Procedural materials for accurate surface textures
  • HDRI lighting for natural environment reflections
  • Render passes composited for post-production control

Development Process

The workflow began with CAD plan conversion and site measurement analysis. After establishing the base structure, I focused on:

  1. Architectural detailing (windows, railings, roofing)
  2. Material definition and UV mapping
  3. Daylight system setup for accurate shadows
  4. Render optimization for quality/efficiency balance
