Logo Design Projects

Brand identity creations for businesses

Project Overview

This collection showcases my logo design work created for various businesses. Each logo was designed to communicate the brand's identity while being versatile across different applications.

Design Process

  • Client consultation to understand brand values
  • Market research and competitor analysis
  • Sketching and conceptualization
  • Digital rendering in Illustrator
  • Presentation of multiple concepts
  • Refinement based on feedback
  • Delivery of final files and brand guidelines

Technical Details

All logos were created as vector graphics in Adobe Illustrator, ensuring scalability. Designs consider:

  • Visual balance and proportion
  • Color psychology
  • Typography pairing
  • Versatility across media
  • Reproduction at small sizes
