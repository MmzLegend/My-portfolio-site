Bowling Alley 3D Models

Detailed assets for bowling environments

Project Overview

This collection features detailed 3D models of bowling alley assets, including balls, pins, shoes, and environment pieces. The models were created with game development and architectural visualization in mind.

Technical Details

  • Modeled in Blender with proper topology
  • High and low poly versions available
  • PBR materials for realistic rendering
  • UV unwrapped for texture painting
  • Optimized for real-time rendering

Creation Process

Each model began with reference gathering and measurement analysis. I focused on creating accurate proportions while maintaining efficient polygon counts.

The texturing process involved creating detailed material definitions that capture the unique surfaces of bowling equipment - from the glossy balls to the wooden lanes and synthetic shoe materials.
