Photography Collection

Capturing school life

Project Overview

This collection captures the vibrant and unfiltered moments from school parties held within student accommodations. Taken in the spirit of genuine student life, these photos reflect the fun, energy, and togetherness of those unforgettable nights.

Technical Details

  • Camera: Nikon D5300 a mid-range DSLR camera
  • Lighting: Natural ambient light only
  • Editing: Minimal retouching in Lightroom & Photoshop
  • Style: Candid, student-style shots with spontaneous framing
  • Settings: Wide aperture, high ISO, fast shutter speed
  • Vibe: Raw, vibrant, and cinematic with real student energy

Creative Process
